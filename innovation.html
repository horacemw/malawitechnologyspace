A fourth year student by the name of sam masikini has developed a voice controlling system which he calls Thandi.the system is able to conduct an intruder detection alert,evidence capturing,facial recognition,voice control,gesture handling,smoke and fire detection,plus appliance control.This system also manages other existing systems installed in the house such as alarms,electric gates and closed circuit televison(CCTV).This prototype is currently designed for household but can be engineered for use by other structures,facilities and persue more complex purposes.With proper financial support from the public and the private sector the project can be scaled up to become a powerful system.Masikini decided to name it Thandi because it is designed to be a helping tool to mankind.

An associate Lecturer in the department of Electrical engineering has developed cervical cancer training kits,theses kits will be used to sharpen,familiarixe and teach medical practioners the skils they should have before going for cervical cancer screening.The cervical cancer training models have been designed in gel-based shape of the cervix that are substantially cheaper,portable and more palatable than plastic or metal models.The kits are being Manufactured at the Malawi polytechnic design studio using locally available resources such as wood,PVC pipes,fabric and Foam.Mia Started to work on his idea when he wasan undergraduate at the polytechnic where hwe studied Bachelor of science in Industrial engineering.However there are commercially available mannequins that help to teach cervical cancer screening but these simulators are costly to be used in developing countries like Malawi.
